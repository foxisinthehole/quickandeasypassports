What is a passport?

A passport is an official travel document issued by the U.S. government that proves your identity and citizenship. It allows you to travel internationally and re-enter the United States after your trip.



How soon can I get an appointment?

We work to secure urgent appointments quickly, often within days, depending on your travel date and documentation.



Do you guarantee same-day passports?

No. Passport issuance and processing times are determined solely by the U.S. Department of State. Our role is to guide you through the preparation and scheduling process to help avoid delays.



How long will my U.S. Passport be valid for?

If you were 16 or older when your passport was issued, it is valid for 10 years. If you were under 16, it is valid for 5 years. Many countries require your passport to be valid for at least 6 months beyond the dates of your travel.



Are there specific requirements for my Passport Photo?

Yes. Passport photos must be 2” x 2”, taken within the past 6 months, and on a plain white background. Photos can be taken at most CVS and Walgreens locations, many of which are open 24/7.



Do children under 16 need a passport?

Yes. Every U.S. citizen, including infants and children, must have their own passport to travel internationally.



What do I need before my appointment?

We provide you with all required forms, a document checklist, and detailed instructions so you arrive prepared and confident.



Where are you located?

Our services are fully remote. You can schedule your appointment and receive all required documents electronically from the comfort of your home.



How do I renew my Passport?

If your passport was issued more than 15 years ago, or when you were under 16, you must apply for a new passport. If it was issued within the last 15 years and you were 16 or older, you may be eligible to renew by mail. We can help determine the right process for your situation and guide you step by step.



Are you affiliated with the government?

No. Quick and Easy Passports is a private, non-government service. We provide administrative support and consultation for passport preparation and scheduling.



How do I book an appointment?

You can request an appointment by filling out the “Schedule Now” form on our website, sending us a direct message, or texting us at 323-909-6637. A team member will contact you to confirm the details.
